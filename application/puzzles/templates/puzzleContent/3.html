Losing Arrangements

Alice and Bob play a game using black and white pieces.
The pieces are arranged into n vertical stacks of size 3.

On Alice's turn, she picks any black piece and removes it, along with any pieces on top.
On Bob's turn, he does the same with white pieces.
If any player is unable to move, they lose.

A given arrangement of pieces is a losing arrangement if both players will lose even if they play optimally.
The number of losing arrangements of size 6 is 7880.
Find the number of losing arrangements of size 1234, and give the last 9 digits of your answer.

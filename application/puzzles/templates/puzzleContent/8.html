The Sieve of Eratosthenes

The sieve of Eratosthenes is an algorithm for finding prime numbers.
A prime number is a number that only has 1 and itself as divisors.
The sieve works by finding primes, and then crossing off all multiples of that prime.

Using this method we can efficiently find primes up to a given limit.
However, while this method suffices for small values, we can actually do better for large values.
Research a more efficient algorithm for solving this problem, and use it to find the sum of all primes under 10987654321.

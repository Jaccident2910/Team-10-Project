Towers of Hanoi 2

This puzzle follows on from Puzzle 1 - Towers of Hanoi.
This time there are four pegs, A, B, C and D.
There are 6 blue disks on peg A and 6 red disks on peg C.
You must move all the blue disks to peg C and all the red disks to peg A.

1. Only the top disk on a rod may be moved at a time.

2. Larger disks may not be placed on top of smaller disks
(however, disks of the same size may be placed on top of each other).


 Your task is to design an algorithm that solves this puzzle in the minimum number of steps.

The algorithm must output a list of moves - for example the list [('A', 'B'), ('B', 'C')]
describes moving the top disk of A to B, and then moving the top disk of B to C.
